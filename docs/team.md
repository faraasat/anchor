## Personal Philosophy

### Why I Build

I believe **software should enhance human capability, not replace human agency**. Too many productivity apps become digital taskmasters, adding stress instead of reducing it.

Anchor is my attempt to build technology that respects:
- **Your time**: Notifications only when contextually relevant
- **Your energy**: Burnout detection instead of hustle culture
- **Your relationships**: Household collaboration without surveillance
- **Your privacy**: Local storage option, minimal data collection

### Influences
- **Cal Newport** (Deep Work, Digital Minimalism): Intentionality over optimization
- **Dieter Rams** (10 Principles of Good Design): Simplicity and honesty in design
- **Basecamp/37signals** (Shape Up): Small teams, big impact
- **Indie Hackers Community**: Sustainable businesses over unicorn dreams
